We are Frutikha

Our journey began in 1990 as a small family-run fruit shop in the heart of the local market. With a passion for fresh, high-quality produce, we built strong relationships with local farmers. Over the years, we’ve grown while staying true to our roots—providing the community with the freshest fruits and friendly service.


Today, we continue delivering fresh fruits while embracing modern convenience through our online platform.
